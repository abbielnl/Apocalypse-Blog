Top 10 Must-Have Items for Your Bug-Out Bag

Posted on April 22, 2025

  1. Water Filter: Clean water is life. A LifeStraw or Sawyer Mini will save you.
  2. Multi-tool: One tool to rule them all—knife, screwdriver, can opener, you name it.
  3. First Aid Kit: Apocalypse doesn’t care about your paper cuts... or your broken arm.
  4. Flashlight & Extra Batteries: Darkness is not your friend.
  5. Fire Starter: Matches, lighters, ferro rod—whatever helps you make fire.
  6. Emergency Blanket: Lightweight, heat-reflective, life-saving tinfoil magic.
  7. Duct Tape: Fixes gear, bandages wounds, makes makeshift shoes if needed.
  8. Portable Food: High-calorie bars, nuts, jerky (or veggie options!).
  9. Map & Compass: GPS won't help when satellites fall out of the sky.
  10. Rope or Paracord: Tie things, climb things, make traps. Endless uses.

Remember: your bug-out bag is your lifeline. Keep it light, essential, and always ready to grab. More tips coming soon!
