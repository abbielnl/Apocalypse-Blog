How to Purify Water in the Wild

Posted on April 22, 2025

Water, water everywhere—but not a drop to drink? Here's how to fix that before your kidneys file a complaint.

  • Boiling: Bring water to a rolling boil for at least 1 minute (3+ at higher altitudes). Kills most bacteria and viruses.
  • Filtration: Use a portable filter like LifeStraw, Sawyer, or a homemade sand/charcoal filter.
  • Purification Tablets: Iodine or chlorine dioxide tabs are small but mighty. Wait time: 30 mins to 4 hours.
  • UV Light: Portable UV purifiers can zap microbes in seconds. Sci-fi level stuff!
  • Solar Disinfection (SODIS): Fill clear plastic bottles and leave them in direct sunlight for 6+ hours. Simple, no tools needed.

Whatever method you choose, pre-filter dirty water through cloth to remove debris first. Better paranoid than poisoned.
