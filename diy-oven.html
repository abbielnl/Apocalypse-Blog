Building a DIY Solar Oven

Posted on April 22, 2025

No power? No problem. Here's how to use the sun to cook your meals and impress the local squirrel tribe.

You’ll Need:

  • A cardboard box (shoebox or pizza box)
  • Aluminum foil (reflects heat)
  • Plastic wrap (creates greenhouse effect)
  • Black paper (absorbs heat)
  • Tape and scissors

How to Build It:

  1. Line the inside with black paper.
  2. Cover the lid or flap with foil—shiny side out.
  3. Stretch plastic wrap across the opening to trap heat.
  4. Angle the foil to reflect sunlight into the box.
  5. Place food inside. S’mores, soup, apocalypse bread—you name it.

Bonus: Use a thermometer to hit at least 150°F (65°C). Takes time, but works great under direct sunlight!
